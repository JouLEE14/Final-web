What is Green Technology?

Green technology uses clean energy, reduces pollution, and protects the Earth. Here are a few solutions changing the world:

☀️ Solar Panels

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity.

Read more

Solar panels are made of photovoltaic cells that capture sunlight and turn it into energy. They are used on homes, schools, and even satellites.

🚗 Electric Vehicles

Electric cars reduce air pollution and run on clean power instead of gasoline.

🌬️ Wind Turbines

Wind turbines generate electricity from wind without polluting the air.
